When programming, what are some of your triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?

I can relate to some of the triggers of experiencing a fight or flight response when I see others have finished the work while I am stuck, I then just want to give up. The thoughts take over "why am I even doing this", "I can't do this", "everyone is so much smarter than me", "I'm never good at anything". It becomes a self depricating cycle.

How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?

I think it is a good regulating method when you start getting overwhelmed with the work. That the emotions are temporary and there is always a solution to be found within life and programming, they go hand in hand.
