What is grid based design?

Grid based design is the alignment of content on the webpage that is evenly distributed by the size of each content on the webpages to make it easy for the viewer to follow.

Whats all the hype about responsive webpages?

Responsive web pages are designed to adapt to all different devices and screen sizes. They automatically resize content depending on the device and screen size to keep easy readibilty for the viewer.

What is semantic structure?

Semantic structure is the baseline in which the viewer can follow the content on the page, naturally people read from top to right and can make out the header as the title and the body as the content. It's used to give the viewer direction throughout the webpage.

Any other points you found interesting and want to reflect on?

It's interesting to see the psychology behind the viewer in which is best for them to read the webpage and keep their attention.
